Welcome to X-Team!

What is the world made of? This is a fundamental question for human being.

As particle physicist, we want to find out the answers by building colliders and particle detectors.

The new physics beyond the Standard Model might have connection to cosmology such as dark matter.

In X-Team, our goal is to build modern detector to search for new physics.

To join us for this exciting journey, feel free to contact any team members!
